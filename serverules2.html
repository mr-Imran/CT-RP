Basic Rules
1. RDM: RDM's full form is Random Death Match. The Definition of RDM's is killing someone for no proper reason and no proper RP. Example - Killing someone, so that you can steal stuff from them. Section 2.1 2. VDM: VDM's full form is Vehicle Deathmatch. VDM is like RDM but the difference is, VDM is done by killing some by vehicle intentionally without any proper reason or without proper RP. Example - When in a drive-by, you see an enemy standing and you crush them with your vehicle is VDM. Section 2.3 3. Meta Gaming: Meta Gaming is Using the information which is not learned from RP and using it in RP. (In our server, if someone do report about you playing roleplay in discord channel and show proper evidence then it'll be considered as meta & it's a ban able offence) Example - Getting to know where someone is by watching their stream. Section 2.4 4. Power Gaming: Power gaming's definition of using In-game mechanics is Power Gaming. Example - • Going to random people and cuffing them without any warnings or reason. • Game modding is totally restricted but visual mods that do not provide any illegal advantages can be done. • Taser guns cannot be used to puncture tires. Without a proper and valid reason, you cannot use a Taser gun on someone continuously. • When you are downed by someone or handcuffed you cannot use radio or phone to communicate. Section 2.5 5. Fail-RP: Doing things that are not possible in real life to make the RP is Fail-Rp. Example - Using the radio when you are dead. Section 2.6 6. Fear-RP: Having the fear of losing life and having a value of life in any situation is known as Fear Rp. Example- /e hands up when life is in threat by people who gun pointed at you. Section 2.7 7. Combat Logging : Combat Logging is Logging out or quitting from the game when in a situation is Combat Logging. ExampleLogging out when in police chase or fight with others. Section 2.8 8. New life Rules : New Life rule means,when you respawn at hospital after dying or get headshot multiple times after dying, you'll not remember the story that led you to your Death. Example- • When in an active situation you cannot respawn or call an EMS on the situation. You can only call an EMS when you are totally alone and you are not injured by another person.Four Men Rules • Criminal activity should not involve more than 4 men. • Information senders will also be counted under 4 men rules. • If you are going to start a situation by your own will against more than 4 the opponent group can engage with all the men power they already have in that certain place. They do not have to drop their members. (edited) [8:57 PM] Section 3.0 Important United Roleplay Guidelines . Permanent Death • Permanent death can only be claimed when you ask for it and your character is injured, and Permanent death should be conducted via /me. Example - /me pulse is dropping /me heavy bleeding continues following pulse drop. /me pulse rate 0 Point to be noted, RP cannot be continued by a character that has been through permanent death. • Permanent death cannot be forced upon someone. About emergency vehicle and emergency services • Under any circumstances without permission, any general citizen cannot ride or hotwire any emergency vehicle. It is considered as a bannable offence. • Roleplaying as a Corrupted Cop or Corrupted EMS Personnel is not allowed. • A situation ends where you get cuffed by the Law Enforcing Department and brought back to Pillbox or PD. By any means there is no chance of starting a situation just to get you out of custody. For example: taking a hostage to demand you let go off the custody.Robbing & Bank/Jewelry/Store Robbery • A valid reason should be on hand to rob any person. Robbing without a proper reason is strictly prohibited. • After finishing a robbery situation you must wait for the law enforcement team to reach the situation. You cannot flee off the situation without conducting proper situations with law enforcement. • Robbery or code red situations cannot be started after the 30 minutes warning of Tsunami. But the running situations can and has to be continued. • In case of bank and jewelry robbery has to follow 4 men rules. • You must have a hostage who is not friendly. If found your hostage is friendly or you don’t have a hostage, officers can perform a breaching action. • In case of store robbery, robbers can have only 3 men and as a hostage you have to kidnap a LEO or Citizen and it is a must. Then you have to do a proper roleplay to call the officers to negotiate. Point to be noted that you can only demand 2 things. • Remember that you have only TWO DEMANDS and both has to be proper and reasonable. Criminal-LEO RP & Interaction• LEO’s can be kidnapped but have to be done via proper RP. But you cannot kidnap a LEO serving an active situation.
